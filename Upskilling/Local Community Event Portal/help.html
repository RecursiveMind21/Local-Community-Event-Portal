❓ Help & Support Center

Everything you need to know about using the Community Portal

📝 How to Register for Events

  1. Click on "Register" in the navigation menu or click the "Register Now" card on the homepage
  2. Fill in your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  3. Select your preferred event type and date
  4. Add any special requests in the feedback section
  5. Click "Submit Registration" to confirm
  6. You'll receive a confirmation message and email

📍 Finding Nearby Events

  1. On the homepage, click the "Find Events" button on the location card
  2. Allow location access when prompted by your browser
  3. Your coordinates will be displayed along with nearby event suggestions
  4. Events are typically within 1-2 miles of your location

💾 Saving Your Preferences

  1. When you select an event type on the registration page, it's automatically saved
  2. Refresh the page and your preferred event will be pre-selected
  3. Use the "Clear Preferences" button to remove saved data
  4. Preferences are stored locally in your browser

🎬 Video Features

  1. Watch event highlight videos on the Events Gallery page
  2. Videos will show "Ready to play" message when loaded
  3. Use video controls to play, pause, and adjust volume
